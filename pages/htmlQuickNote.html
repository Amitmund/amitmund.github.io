For responsive image in bootstrap, use img-fluid class. 
Example:


<img class="img-fluid" src="/images/samvritaInFog.jpg" alt="samvritaInFog">

Output:





 

At this example, we have provided the size:


<img class="img-fluid" src="/images/samvritaInFog.jpg" alt="samvritaInFog" width="460" height="345">

<img class="img-fluid" src="/images/samvritaInFog.jpg"" alt="samvritaInFog" width="auto" height="auto">
